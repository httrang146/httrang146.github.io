Project Overview

This project involved developing a comprehensive and interactive Power BI Inventory Tracking Dashboard to monitor and analyze inventory performance. The dashboard enables stakeholders to quickly assess inventory health, understand sales trends, track fluctuations in Sell-in prices, manage vendors, and make informed replenishment decisions.

By simulating inventory management scenarios using sample retail data, the project demonstrates practical insights for supply chain optimization.

Objective

  • Build an interactive dashboard to monitor credit risk exposure, identify high-risk customers, and support data-driven decisions for credit portfolio management.
  • The dashboard focuses on payment behavior, delinquency patterns, and risk segmentation to help stakeholders proactively manage credit risk.

Context

The dashboard was built using the Credit Risk Classification Dataset by Praveen, which simulates a Customer Transaction and Demographic related data, including Risky and Not Risky customer for specific banking products. The data contains customer-level information on:

  • Payment behavior (on-time vs overdue)
  • Delinquency severity (1‚Äì30 DPD, 31‚Äì60 DPD, 60+/90+ DPD)
  • Credit utilization and exposure

Role

Data Analyst / Credit Risk Analyst

  • Performed data understanding, cleaning, and validation
  • Engineered credit risk features (delinquency ratios, exposure metrics, behavioral indicators)
  • Segmented customers into risk groups using rule-based logic
  • Designed and built an interactive dashboard for portfolio-level and customer-level analysis
  • Translated analytical findings into actionable risk insights and recommendations

    • This role allowed me to combine data analysis, visualization, and business understanding to provide a practical tool for credit risk team.

Tools and Methodologies

  • Tools: Tableau (Dashboard design)
  • Methodologies: Credit Risk Feature Engineering, Risk Segmentation, Portfolio Analysis
